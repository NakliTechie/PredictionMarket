HOW TO USE

🎯 What is this?

A simulator to understand how prediction markets work. Control all 4 players (A, B, C, D) to simulate a small market.

📊 Parimutuel Mode

All players place bets into a shared pool. After betting closes, the market is resolved.

  • Winning bets split the entire pool proportionally
  • Payout = stake × (total pool / total on winning side)
  • Losing bets get nothing

Example: If $1000 is staked on YES and $500 on NO, YES bettors split $1500. Each $1 on YES gets $1.50 back.

📈 LMSR Mode

Continuous market with dynamic prices. Each trade changes the odds immediately.

  • LMSR = Logarithmic Market Scoring Rule
  • Prices move based on quantity purchased
  • Liquidity (b) controls how much prices move per trade
  • Buy shares of YES or NO; each share pays $1 if correct

Example: Buying 10 YES shares might cost $5.50. If YES wins, those shares redeem for $10. If NO wins, they're worth $0.

🎮 How to Demo

  1. Set up the question (Yes/No or Two Options)
  2. Choose a mode (Parimutuel or LMSR)
  3. Click "Create Market"
  4. Select a player (A/B/C/D)
  5. Place bets or execute trades
  6. Watch odds/payouts update in real-time
  7. Resolve the market to see final results

💡 Tips

  • Use different players to simulate diverse opinions
  • In LMSR, larger trades move prices more
  • Lower liquidity (b) = more volatile prices
  • Reset anytime by creating a new market
